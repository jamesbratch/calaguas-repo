About Us

Arrive as Guests, Leave As Friends!

Calaguas White Sand Resort is a beautiful tropical island resort with kilometers of white sand beaches, palm trees, and lush, green mountains. The warm waters, white sand beaches, and mountain hiking trails will provide lasting memories and Calaguas White Sand Resort will become one of your favorite vacation getaways.
